About Ammayapan Charitable Trust

Since its inception, Ammayapan Charitable Trust has been dedicated to uplifting the underprivileged through various social initiatives. Our mission is to provide shelter, medical care, and emotional support to those in need, particularly elderly women who have no other means of support.

Our home operates in Thiruvannamalai, focusing on holistic development and well-being for the elderly and marginalized communities. Through donations and volunteer efforts, we aim to make a significant impact and bring hope to those in need.
